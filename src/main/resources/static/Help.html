Help & Support

1. How do I register as a student or tutor?

Click on the respective “Student Register” or “Tutor Register” link in the top navigation bar and fill out the form with accurate information.

2. How do I find tutors near me?

After registering as a student and logging in, you can search for tutors based on location, subjects, and grade level.

3. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click the “Login” button and then select “Forgot Password” to reset it via your registered email.

4. How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and go to the Profile section. You can edit your details and save the changes.

5. Still need help?

If you have any other questions or issues, please contact us here or email us at tutorbuddy110@gmail.com.
